How It Works

A seamless process, from booking to delivery.

01

Consultation

We discuss your property, timeline, deliverable requirements, and the specific shots that matter most to your listing or project goals.

02

Pre-Shoot Prep

I provide a staging checklist so the space is camera-ready on arrival — maximising time on-site and minimising re-shoots.

03

The Shoot

Careful composition, precise lighting, and methodical technique produce frames that need minimal correction — just refinement.

04

Delivery

Professionally retouched, web and print-ready files delivered within 24–48 hours via private gallery, with full licensing for your intended use.
